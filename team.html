I am an Assistant Professor in the Artificial Intelligence Institute (AIISC) and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of South Carolina (USC). I am also affiliated with the Carolina Autism and Neurodevelopment (CAN) research center. Before joining USC, I was a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis (WashU). My past affiliations are: 1. Applied Mathematics Lab @WashU; 2. Brain Dynamics and Control Research Group @WashU; and 3. Embedded Control Systems and Networking Laboratory @MST.
